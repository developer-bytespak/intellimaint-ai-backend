# Lezyne CNC Dirt Floor Drive O-Rings Replacement

If your pump seems to be unable to fill tires to as high of a pressure as should, it may be due to leaking O-rings. Use this guide to replace your faulty O-rings.

## 1. Step 1
- Detach the hose from its holder.
![Step 1 Image 321375](https://guide-images.cdn.ifixit.com/igi/CTNSMRJKYgjrlKMG.full)
<!-- Image URLs: thumbnail=https://guide-images.cdn.ifixit.com/igi/CTNSMRJKYgjrlKMG.thumbnail, medium=https://guide-images.cdn.ifixit.com/igi/CTNSMRJKYgjrlKMG.medium, large=https://guide-images.cdn.ifixit.com/igi/CTNSMRJKYgjrlKMG.large, original=https://guide-images.cdn.ifixit.com/igi/CTNSMRJKYgjrlKMG.full -->

## 2. Step 2
- Hold the base of the pump steady and twist the barrel counter-clockwise by hand until it completely unthreads.
> **Note:** If you are having troubles stabilizing the base and twisting the barrel, try standing on the base and using both hands to loosen the barrel.
- Lift the barrel off the base.
> **Note:** When removing the barrel, be sure not to lose the bump stop O-ring. It may stick to the pump base, may be stuck inside the barrel, or may fall out when you remove the barrel.

## 3. Step 3
- Lift the pump handle until the top cap is exposed.
![Step 3 Image 321378](https://guide-images.cdn.ifixit.com/igi/WvPUYGWjLpnW1ZMY.full)
<!-- Image URLs: thumbnail=https://guide-images.cdn.ifixit.com/igi/WvPUYGWjLpnW1ZMY.thumbnail, medium=https://guide-images.cdn.ifixit.com/igi/WvPUYGWjLpnW1ZMY.medium, large=https://guide-images.cdn.ifixit.com/igi/WvPUYGWjLpnW1ZMY.large, original=https://guide-images.cdn.ifixit.com/igi/WvPUYGWjLpnW1ZMY.full -->

## 4. Step 4
- Place the ends of needle nose pliers into the top cap holes and turn counter-clockwise until the cap is loose.
- Finish removing the top cap by hand.
- Pull the pump plunger out of the barrel.

## 5. Step 5
- Use a small flathead screwdriver to remove the plunger O-ring.
- Use the screwdriver to remove the upper bump stop O-ring.
- Install  new O-rings.
> ⚠️ **Caution:** Be careful not to over-stretch or cut the O-rings if you are using the screwdriver to install them.

## 6. Step 6
- Remove the lower bump stop O-ring from the pump base.
- Remove and replace the base O-ring.
![Step 6 Image 321384](https://guide-images.cdn.ifixit.com/igi/upGaxQQnMFXVXJhT.full)

## 7. Step 7
- Insert the tips of needle nose pliers into the base cap holes and turn it counter-clockwise until it completely unthreads.
- Remove the base cap from the base.
- Remove and replace the base cap O-ring.
- Re-assemble the pump using these instructions backwards.
  - Don't forget to set a new bump stop O-ring on top of the pump base before re-installing the pump barrel.
