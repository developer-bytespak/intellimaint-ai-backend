## Model Identification


This manual covers generator set models produced under the Cummins brand.

To avoid errors or delay in filling your parts order, always state the MODEL and SERIAL NUMBER from the
Cummins nameplate.

The engine serial number is located on a data tag on the engine or is stamped on the engine block.

The control software number is located on the electronic control module (ECM) or can be obtained via the
digital interface if one is available.
